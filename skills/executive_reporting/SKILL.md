# Executive Advisory Reporting Skill

**Goal**: Synthesize multi-dimensional business diagnostics into a final premium advisory report.

**Steps**:
1. Gather and review sub-agent findings from episodic or working memories.
2. Call the `generate_artifact` tool passing the gathered findings.
3. Polish and format headings, blockquotes, tables, and alerts in the report.
4. Supplement findings with actionable strategic recommendation points.
